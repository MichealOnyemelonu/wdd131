Stress Relief Techniques

  • Take slow, deep breaths for 5 minutes
  • Go for a short walk outside
  • Write down what is worrying you

Mindfulness Practices

Mindfulness helps you stay present and calm.

  • Focus on your breathing
  • Notice sounds around you
  • Observe thoughts without judging them

Healthy Daily Routines

  • Eat regular, balanced meals
  • Drink enough water
  • Take short breaks during work or study

Sleep Improvement Tips

  • Go to bed at the same time every day
  • Avoid screens before sleeping
  • Create a calm bedtime routine
