## Mental Health & Self‑Care Tips Website

### Subject

Mental Health & Self‑Care Tips Website

### Content (Brief Description)

This website will provide simple, practical mental health and self‑care resources. It will include pages on stress‑relief techniques, mindfulness practices, healthy routines, sleep improvement, and positive habits. The site will also feature an interactive JavaScript “Daily Mood Tracker” or “Daily Self‑Care Reminder,” where users can log their mood and receive tips. It will be responsive and built using valid HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to demonstrate web development skills

### Reason

I chose this subject because mental health has become a major concern worldwide, especially as people deal with busy schedules, work pressure, and digital overload. A clear and supportive resource can help others improve their well‑being. It is also a topic I care about personally, and it gives me a meaningful way to apply my web development skills while creating something useful and relevant in today’s world.
