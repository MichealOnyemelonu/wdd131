Website Site Plan

Site Name

Mental Health & Self-Care Tips

The site name was chosen because it clearly explains the focus of the website. It reflects a supportive and informative space where users can find practical tips to improve their mental well-being and daily self-care habits.

Optional domain: selfcaretips.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide simple, practical mental health and self-care resources. The site will include information about stress relief, mindfulness, healthy routines, sleep improvement, and positive habits. It will also feature a small interactive tool such as a daily mood tracker or self-care reminder.

Scenarios

  • How can I reduce stress after a long or busy day?
  • What simple self-care habits can I add to my daily routine?

Color Schema

  • #2c6e63 (Dark Green): Used for headings and titles
  • #6bbfb5 (Soft Teal): Used for accents and borders
  • #f2f7f5 (Light Mint): Used for the page background

Typography

  • Arial / Helvetica (sans-serif): Used for body text
  • Bold Arial: Used for headings and section titles
